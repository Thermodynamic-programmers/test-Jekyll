About The Stock

5 min read
Updated Dec 2024

The Stock is a community-driven encyclopedia dedicated to documenting the best software, services, and tools available on the internet.

Open Source
The Stock is completely open-source. You can contribute articles, fix errors, or suggest new entries on our GitHub repository.

Our Mission

We believe that the best tools should be accessible to everyone. The internet is full of incredible software, but discovering it can be overwhelming.

What We Cover

  • Communication tools — Messengers, VoIP, forums
  • Productivity software — Note-taking, task management, wikis
  • Development tools — IDEs, containers, CLI utilities
